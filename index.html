📜 Rights & Rules of the Ranch Chat 🐂

This is the Ranch Chat. It ain’t perfect, it ain’t corporate. It’s just ours. It’s here to build trust,
share laughs, make plans, and strengthen the crew. So here are the simple rules to keep it solid,
safe, and respectful for everybody.

📜 Privacy & Respect

💬 “The right words at the right time build bridges. The wrong ones burn them.”
Let’s be the ones who build.
  • No using someone’s phone number outside this chat without their permission.
  • If they didn’t give you their number personally, don’t call or text them privately.

💬 Conduct in the Chat

  • Don’t be hateful, racist, sexist, or belittle people.
  • We all cuss, but don’t be graphic or disrespectful — know the line.
  • You can joke and roast — just keep it light, not cruel.
  • If someone crosses a line, handle it in private.
  • Reprimand in private, praise in public.

🧠 Thoughts

  • If you’re not sure if something’s okay to say… it probably isn’t.
  • Nobody here is perfect. That’s cool—we’re learning.
  • This isn’t a group to control anybody. It’s a group to respect everybody.

🚫 What the Chat is NOT For

  • This is NOT where you call out of work — you MUST call the store or Kelly directly.
  • This IS where you can offer up shifts or ask for coverage.

🧒 We Got Youth Here

  • We’ve got minors in this chat. Keep the vibe PG-13.
  • Cussing is okay. Graphic or explicit stuff is not.
  • This is a crew, not a locker room. Let’s show the younger folks how to carry themselves right.

    • 🧭 Behavior Code

    • Light roasting is fine if it’s mutual. But hateful or targeted speech = not cool.
    • You can say, “you’re a dick.” You can’t say, “go suck a dick.” ➤ There’s a difference, and we all know it.
    • Don’t belittle people’s age, gender, race, religion, or background—we’re here to lift, not crush.

🤝 Equal Ground

  • No judging based on age, sex, race, religion, or personal views.
  • If you don’t vibe with someone, just don’t hang out — no need for drama.

    • Personal Boundaries

    • Light flirting is fine if it’s mutual.
    • Dudes: be aware of your tone.
    • No one should feel unsafe or uncomfortable because of a chat message. Period.

🙏 Belief & Expression

  • You’re welcome to say “God bless,” “amen,” or “thank you, Jesus” if that’s how you feel.
  • But no preaching or political debates—we keep that separate from this space.
  • You be you—but don’t try to convert or correct anyone else for being themselves.

📝 Strike System?

  • If someone keeps crossing lines, we’ll figure it out.
  • Kelly is the manager. Any real enforcement or consequences come down to her call.
  • For now: just don’t be a jerk, and we won’t have to deal with strikes.
Just because someone doesn’t want to hang with you doesn’t mean they hate you. Respect their space. Don’t take it personal — they’re just protecting their peace. Don’t step on someone’s happiness just because you feel left out.
